Monaco Release

AMP’s new release – Monaco – powers CRE management productivity

Features you’ve requested.
Brought to you in AMP’s new release, Monaco.

AMP’s new release, codenamed Monaco, comes with features that you always needed, but never had, further enhancing the premier commercial real estate asset management product. From enterprise social extension to multi-building support, AMP’s latest features will definitely excite you. Let’s raise the curtains on them!

Manage Your Tasks Better

Experience AMP’s powerful collaboration features extended into the Receivables page as well. Comments alert team members as soon as they are posted and enterprise social features having everyone in the organization on the same page.

AMP now provides support to multi-building projects. Multiple buildings forming part of a single portfolio or assets that are part of a single project can be grouped together and analyzed for better clarity. What was a challenge on other platforms is now possible on AMP.
New filters within the Receivables page enable you to view tenants with only unpaid amounts, or with unpaid or prepaid balances. Each toggle allows you to see what the average rent looks like with that choice – realtime!
Receivables can now be exported as a PDF. The PDFs are carefully formatted and retain the familiar and user-friendly look of the platform. Log on to AMP the next time you need materials for presentations and reports.
  • Additional features in the Administrative Panel
  • Better clarity of totals in the Rent Roll page
