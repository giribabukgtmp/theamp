AMP Lease

AMPLease is the world's most collaborative and efficient lease management platform with data integration capabilities built-in. Offering the ability to create and manage comprehensive lease abstracts, AMPLease provides insight into a property or portfolio's occupancy/vacancy and lease analytics on a level never seen before in commercial real estate.

Seamlessly integrate from any leasing platform for more efficient lease management. Take charge of everything from expirations, occupancy levels, vacancies, new leases, and leases that have not yet commenced.

Commercial leasing made easy

  • AMPLease allows you to create new leases and manage already existing leases with exhaustive lease abstract data capture.
  • AMPLease seamlessly integrates with your system, providing effortless visibility into lease level data. AMPLease provides effective and easy to comprehend lease analytics.
  • Identify expirations exposure by factors such as property, tenant, portfolio, geography, asset class or manager.
