Miami Release

AMP Technologies has combined the power of the revolutionary Asset Management Platform and the best leasing CRM app with the new Miami Release to create the most power asset management software for commercial real estate that is truly international




Companies work tirelessly to overcome the obstacles of the different nuances between locations when conducting business. Time change, communication, measurement, all play a role in the strategy of international companies. The Miami Release answers this call with new additions to the Asset Management Platform giving users the ability to grow their companies at a rapid pace without the hiccups that often come with crossing borders. Neel Naicker, CEO and Co-Founder “Commercial Real Estate spans the entire globe encompassing trillions of dollars. CEO’s need a tool that allows them to lead an internationally minded company while still focusing in on regions and local settings; AMP is that solution”.
