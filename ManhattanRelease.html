Manhattan Release

AMP Unveils IRR and Valuation Calculators in 2015’s Most Important Software Release for Commercial Real Estate




Quite simply we have considered how Commercial Real Estate companies make money now and how they plan for a successful future; and we've provided easy solutions for both . - Neel Naicker, CEO/Co-Founder

AMP Technologies’ Manhattan Release is the most important upgrade in Asset Management software since AMP’s inception in 2010. By Placing an IRR and Valuation calculator directly on the home dashboard, AMP has taken the guesswork out of the value of properties and portfolios, and made making smart investments easier.

Manhattan, the epicenter of the world’s most exciting city, and of the Commercial Real Estate world is the theme for the most exciting and most important software release in Commercial Real Estate. AMP has combined the power of its revenue building Asset Management Platform with an Internal Rate of Return calculator and a Valuation Calculator to give users access to unlimited growth potential.–

By accurately calculating the Internal Rate of Return on a potential investment, Commercial Real Estate professionals can gauge the future success and return of multiple investment options to choose which avenue will be more profitable within a business model. AMP has placed a new IRR Calculator directly on the home dashboard giving users direct access to smart decision making for their future.

The Manhattan Release also brings with it a new Valuation Calculator. AMP has combined the revenue building Asset Management Platform with the Valuation Calculator gaining access to unprecedented decision making potential. No longer will Commercial Real Estate companies struggle through complicated valuation processes, but instead AMP users have access to the value of their properties and portfolios in one simple thirty second view

The excitement of the Manhattan Release does not end at an industry changing IRR Calculator or a disruptive Valuation Calculator. Manhattan also announces that all assets on AMP will be displayed with stunning Bing Maps. This marks the perfect marriage of the best in geolocation technology and the best in asset management software. Manhattan, quite simply, is the biggest thing to hit Commercial Real Estate in 2015.
