Finance & Accounting

AMP's Finance and Accounting Service Delivery Team is comprised of chartered accountants (equivalent CPAs) and accounting graduates, trained in property and fund accounting. Our accountants specialize in all commercial real estate asset classes including specialty retail, office, industrial, multifamily, among other asset classes.

We provide a cost effective and scalable back office property accounting solution for our clients. Our accounting services range from month end close, accounts payable, account receivables, among other property and fund accounting services. AMP's Finance and Accounting Services solution helps clients reduce their operating costs by anywhere between 50-60%.
